I have been taking photos as a hobby and way to express my art since the age of 11 years old. But not until high school did I begin to learn the process of film photography at an afterschool program at the International Center of Photography and developing the rolls in a darkroom. I fell in love and once in a blue I use my old Minolta my father gave me to shoot some black and white film... sadly I have no money or space to develop the film myself anymore.
